# Product Requirements Document

## Vision
ScholarIQ helps students understand how they learn, not just what they study.

## Target Users
- College students
- Competitive exam aspirants
- Lifelong learners

## MVP
- Authentication
- Study session CRUD
- Goals
- Dashboard
- Analytics
- AI study coach

## Stretch Goals
- PDF chat (RAG)
- Flashcards
- Quiz generation
- Calendar sync
- Email reminders
